# GreenPulse: Carbon Footprint Assistant & Eco Advisor

**GreenPulse** is a modern, interactive web application designed to help users measure, track, and actively reduce their daily carbon footprint. Combining social gamification, real-time tracking, and advanced AI advisor integrations, GreenPulse transforms ecological awareness into a collaborative and rewarding experience.

View your app in AI Studio: https://ai.studio/apps/02d24349-6ae5-4cf7-9a17-d58592c0b8b7

---

## Key Features

- **AI Environmental Advisor (ADK 2.0)**: Powered by Google's Gemini-2.5-flash and the Agent Development Kit (ADK), our AI advisor answers climate queries and dynamically logs carbon-reducing actions from conversation.
- **Eco Dashboard & Daily Logger**: Log habits across food, transit, waste, and energy. Track your real-time carbon reduction (kg CO₂e) and accumulate EcoPoints.
- **Social Feed & AI Caption Generator**: Share updates, comment on community posts, and use AI to generate engaging social captions for your accomplishments.
- **Share Card Creator**: Design and export gorgeous customizable cards (minimalist, terminal, glassmorphism) illustrating your carbon-reduction achievements.
- **Gamified Leaderboards**: Compete with others in footprint reductions and overall EcoPoints, and automatically earn digital rank/achievement badges.
- **Carbon Scanner**: Scan items to estimate carbon footprint impact and log green activities on the fly.
